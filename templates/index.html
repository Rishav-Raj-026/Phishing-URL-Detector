What is phishing?

Phishing is a cyber attack that uses disguised email addresses, websites, or messages to steal personal information. The attackers masquerade as a trusted entity and trick users into opening malicious links or providing sensitive data.

About PhishGuard

Our Mission

PhishGuard is dedicated to helping users stay safe online by identifying potentially malicious websites before they can cause harm. Our platform uses advanced analysis techniques and machine learning to detect phishing attempts.

How It Works
  1. Enter a suspicious URL in our analyzer
  2. Our system extracts dozens of features from the URL and website
  3. Our machine learning model analyzes these features for phishing indicators
  4. You receive a detailed report with the risk assessment and explanation
Features We Analyze
  • URL length and structure
  • Domain age and registration details
  • Use of IP addresses or suspicious TLDs
  • Presence of HTTPS/SSL
  • Redirects and forwarding
  • Presence of suspicious keywords
  • Website content and behavior
  • External resource loading
Stay Protected Online

Phishing remains one of the most common cyber attacks. Always verify the URLs you visit, be suspicious of unsolicited emails, and use tools like PhishGuard to check questionable links before clicking.

Important Note

While we strive for accuracy, no phishing detection tool is 100% perfect. Always exercise caution with sensitive information online.
